What can humans do to help prevent the problem of animal extinction?

Here are a few habits and tips, among many others, that can make a difference!

  • Learn about endangered species in your area.
  • Recycle if possible and buy sustainable products (preferably local produce).
  • Resist from purchasing products made from threatened or endangered species (food,fashion items etc.).
  • Don't litter.
  • Do not use toxic herbicides/pesticides.
  • Spread the word about endangered species with others.
  • Cut down on plastics.
  • Grow native plants.
